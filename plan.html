Goals

The goal is to develop a website that appeals to a younger target audience and makes them want to be involved with what CossiePlay has to offer.

By appealing to a younger audience, both crowd numbers and revenue will increase.
The website will be developed in a simple and clear manner that will reduce the need for "page hopping" so therefore the message of what Cossieplay
has to offer is quickly delivered.

 

Success Evaluation

The sites success can be monitored by the number of Sign-Up's or Store enquiries that have been requested/mailed.

As the site does offer a range of contactable features the customer will have numerous options in order to contact CossiePlay
and therefore success can be measured by what path the customer chooses to use.

Target Audience

Currently CossiePlay is appealing to an older target audience between the ages of 35 to 50.
The goal is to appeal to a younger target audience that ranges from teens to early adolescence (18-23).
However, the site should still be appealing to the older target audience in order to not lose current customers
